Bernard Binlin Dadié Poetry Digital Edition Prototype

This is a prototype for a digital edition of a collection of poetry by Bernard Binlin Dadié, translated by Peggy Wright-Cleveland. The features of this prototype that we are testing out are rendering TEI as HTML, embedded annotation, and more robust searching.
